10D8N North & South Island Private Tour – New Zealand

Auckland – Hobbiton – Rotorua – Taupo – Christchurch – Lake Tekapo – Mt Cook – Queenstown – Milford Sound

📅 Musim: Semi (September–November) • 👥 Private trip – Min. 4 peserta • 🚐 Transportasi: Mini Van beserta driver

🗓️ Rangkuman Itinerary

📅 Hari 1: Jakarta – Transit Sydney

  • Penerbangan malam dari Jakarta dengan Qantas
  • Transit di Sydney – lanjut ke Auckland
  • Bermalam di pesawat

📅 Hari 2: Tiba di Auckland – City Tour

  • Dijemput di bandara Auckland
  • City tour: Sky Tower, Queen Street, Viaduct Harbour
  • 🏨 Hotel: Holiday Inn Express Auckland City Centre ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Auckland

📅 Hari 3: Hobbiton & Rotorua

  • Perjalanan ke Matamata – tur Hobbiton Movie Set
  • Lanjut ke Rotorua – kunjungan ke Government Gardens & Lake Rotorua
  • 🏨 Hotel: Jet Park Hotel Rotorua ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Rotorua

📅 Hari 4: Rotorua – Taupo – Kembali ke Auckland

  • Kunjungan ke Wai-O-Tapu & Huka Falls, Taupo
  • Sore kembali ke Auckland
  • 🏨 Hotel: President Hotel Auckland ★★★
  • Bermalam di Auckland

📅 Hari 5: Auckland – Christchurch

  • Pagi bebas / opsional kunjungan ke Auckland Domain
  • Siang terbang ke Christchurch
  • City tour ringan: Hagley Park, Avon River, Riverside Market
  • 🏨 Hotel: Wyndham Garden Christchurch ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Christchurch

📅 Hari 6: Christchurch – Lake Tekapo – Mt Cook

  • Kunjungan ke Church of the Good Shepherd & Lake Tekapo
  • Photo stop di Mt Cook National Park
  • 🏨 Hotel: The Godley Hotel Tekapo ★★★
  • Bermalam di Tekapo

📅 Hari 7: Tekapo – Queenstown via Lindis Pass

  • Perjalanan scenic via Lindis Pass
  • Waktu bebas di Queenstown tepi danau
  • 🏨 Hotel: Holiday Inn Queenstown Remarkables Park ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Queenstown

📅 Hari 8: Milford Sound Cruise

  • Road trip ke Fiordland National Park
  • Milford Sound Cruise (termasuk lunch box)
  • 🏨 Hotel: Holiday Inn Queenstown Remarkables Park ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Queenstown

📅 Hari 9: Queenstown – Hari Bebas

  • Aktivitas pribadi: belanja, kuliner, foto-foto
  • 🏨 Hotel: Holiday Inn Queenstown Remarkables Park ★★★★
  • Bermalam di Queenstown

📅 Hari 10: Queenstown – Jakarta

  • Transfer ke Bandara Queenstown
  • Penerbangan pulang via Sydney
  • Tiba malam hari di Jakarta
